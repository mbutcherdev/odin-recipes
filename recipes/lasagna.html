Lasagna Recipe

Description:

This is a classic lasagna recipe that is easy to make and tastes great. This is a great recipe to make for a crowd.

Ingredients:

    Sauce:
    • Virgin olive oil
    • 1 pound ground beef
    • 1/2 medium onion (diced)
    • 1/2 bell pepper (diced)
    • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
    • 1 (28-ounce)can good-quality tomato sauce
    • 3 ounces tomato paste (half a 6-ounce can)
    • 1 (14 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
    • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano, or 2 teaspoons dried oregano
    • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (preferably flat leaf), packed
    • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
    • 1 pinch garlic powder and/or garlic salt
    • 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
    • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar (to taste, optional)
    • Salt
    Lasagna:
    • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles (requires 9 lasagna noodles - unbroken)
    • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
    • 1 1/2 pounds mozzarella cheese, grated
    • 1/4 pound freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:

  1. Pasta
    1. Boil a large pot of salter water (1 Tbs per 2 quarts)
    2. Once boiling, add lasagna noodles and cook al dente (check instructions, may be pre-cooked)
    3. Whilst cooking: skip to the 'Beef' section
    4. Drain and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process, set aside
    5. Spread a little olive oil on a large rimmed baking tray, covered with a baking sheet
    6. Spread out the cooked pasta on this sheet, turning each to ensure coating
  2. Beef
    1. Heat a large skillet with 2 teaspoons of olive oil on medium-high
    2. Add the ground beef until lightly brown on all sides
    3. Transfer the beef to a bowl, leaving a tablespoon of fat
    4. Add the diced pepper and onion to the skillet
    5. Cook for 4 - 5 minutes, until peppers are soft and onion is translucent
    6. Add the minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds
    7. Return the beef to the pan, mix well and reduce to low heat for a further 5 minutes
  3. Sauce
    1. Transfer the beef mixture to a medium sized pot, add tomatoes, puree and paste to the pot
    2. Add parsley, oregano and other seasonings, adjust to personal taste. Sprinkle with garlic salt
    3. Sprinkle some red or white wine vinegar, stirring in sugar by the tablespoon (taste after each addition!)
    4. Bring to a simmer, then reduce to low heat and cook for 15-40 minutes, stir often. Ensure nothing sticks to the base of the pot!
    5. Remove from heat
  4. Cooking and serve
    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish
    2. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of the baking dish
    3. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, breaking them to fit if necessary
    4. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese over the noodles, then 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese
    5. Spread 1/3 of the sauce over the cheese, then sprinkle with 1/3 of the Parmesan cheese
    6. Repeat layers twice, ending with a layer of sauce and Parmesan cheese
    7. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 10 minutes if you'd like a crusty top
    8. Allow the lasagna to cool for 10-15 minutes before serving
    9. Enjoy!
  5. Notes:
    • This will keep for around 5 days
    • Keep covered with foil, but try not to let it touch the sauce
    • Reheat in a microwave or oven for a freshly cooked dinner all week!
